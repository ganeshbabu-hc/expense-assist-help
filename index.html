SpendWise Help

To add and track expenses or incomes:
  1. Open the app and navigate to the "Add Transaction" section.
  2. Enter the details such as amount, category, and date.
  3. Save the transaction.
Enable the SMS reading feature to automatically track transactions from your messages:
  1. Go to Settings and enable SMS reading.
  2. Grant necessary permissions for the app to read SMS.
Manage your expenses effectively by setting up budgets:
  1. Access the "Budgets" section in the app.
  2. Set monthly or category-specific budgets.
  3. Monitor your spending against the set budgets.
<> Categorize your transactions for better organization:
  1. Navigate to the "Categories" or "Tags" section.
  2. Add, edit, or delete categories as needed.
  3. Assign categories when adding or editing transactions.
Track different payment methods:
  1. Visit the "Payment Types" section.
  2. Add, edit, or delete payment types like cash, credit card, etc.
  3. Choose the payment type when adding transactions.
